Helping you find the hottest party since 2013.

Created by Max Dietz, Sam Giagtzoglou, and Jack Lashner during the Fall 2013 Wesleyan Hackathon, Wes Party Maps will show you the hotspots on campus all weekend, ensurings that you and and your friends have a good time. It finds the hottest parties by tracking your smartphones every half hour, so please install the apps to get us our precious data. The GPS information is recorded without any identifying information, so it is completely anonymous, but it will ask for you home address so that we know when you are out having a good time and when you're just sleeping.

Check out our code on Github, in case you're the kind of person who likes vertigo.
